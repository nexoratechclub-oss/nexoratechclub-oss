## About

Nexora Tech Club is a community that brings together students, developers, designers, and technology enthusiasts to learn, collaborate, and build together.

Our objective is to create an environment where members continuously improve their technical skills, gain real-world experience, and become industry-ready through teamwork and knowledge sharing.

---

## What We Do

- Technical Workshops
- Coding Sessions
- Hackathons
- Project Collaboration
- Industry Mentorship

---

## Our Focus Areas

- Web Development
- Mobile App Development
- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning
- Data Science
- UI/UX Design

---

## Our Mission

To build a collaborative ecosystem where learning is practical, innovation is encouraged, and every member gets opportunities to grow personally and professionally.

## Join Us

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, Nexora welcomes everyone who is passionate about technology and continuous learning.
